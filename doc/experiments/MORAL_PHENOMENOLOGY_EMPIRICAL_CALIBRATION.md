# Moral Phenomenology — Empirical Calibration

> **Purpose:** Anchor the BPR moral-phenomenology mapping (privation framework
> and exorcism re-coherence reading) to the peer-reviewed empirical record on
> structured-deliverance ritual, cult-exit recovery, dissociative-identity
> disorder treatment, cross-cultural possession, and ritual neuroscience.
> **Method:** Five-angle literature survey (May 2026), preregistered work and
> outcome-trial evidence weighted over narrative reviews.

## 1. The convergent picture

Five independent literatures — Catholic exorcism + psychiatry, cross-cultural
anthropology of possession, ritual neuroscience, DID / Possession-form DID
clinical treatment, and cult-exit / coercive-persuasion recovery — converge
on the same structural elements for recovery from identity-coupling capture:

1. **Voluntary engagement / consent** (universal in modern clinical and
   exit-counseling practice; strong in Catholic/Pentecostal/ruqyah deliverance;
   weaker in zar/Umbanda/Korean kut traditions that integrate rather than expel)
2. **Trained operator / therapist** (universal across all five literatures)
3. **External structural anchors** (sacred objects, ritual formulae, ISSTD
   phase-based protocol, psychoeducational frameworks)
4. **Communal coherence field** (Church, congregation, terreiro, family
   network, therapeutic relationships)
5. **Time and repetition** (universal — months to years, never days)
6. **Integration as recovery state** (DID's ISSTD three-phase ends in
   integration; zar/Umbanda/kut frame recovery as integration; modern
   cult-exit emphasizes reconstruction of integrated pre-group identity;
   expulsion-only framings are a *theological interpretation* of one
   sub-tradition, not the structural invariant)

The convergence across independent traditions and research programs is the
strongest available empirical support for the framework's *structural*
prediction. It is not evidence for any supernatural commitment.

## 3. Framework refinements warranted

### Refinement R-1: Integration ≥ expulsion as the structurally complete recovery model

The original `EXORCISM_BPR_MAPPING.md` emphasized expulsion (reading the Catholic
Rite). The broader literature pushes toward integration as the structurally
complete model:

- **DID treatment** ends in *integration* of identity-states, not expulsion
- **Zar, Umbanda, Korean kut** *integrate* entities, not expel them
- **Cult exit** *reconstructs* pre-group identity rather than purging
- **Carhart-Harris REBUS** describes "re-canalization with revised priors,"
  not removal of foreign content

Crucially, this fits the **privation framework better than expulsion does**:
if evil is privation (no substance), there is nothing to expel. Re-coherencing
brings the disordered identity-states into stable integration with the
person's continuing identity. Expulsion-only framings are one theological
interpretation; integration is the structural invariant across most
traditions and the modern clinical standard.

### Refinement R-2: Consent requirement is empirically vindicated

The framework's claim that permission-field re-gating requires the subject's
internal consent is empirically supported:

- Forced deprogramming relapse rate ~25–33% (Langone)
- Coercive exorcism associated with iatrogenic harm (Rosik 1997, Pfeifer 1994)
- ISSTD and TOP DD treatment protocols require informed consent
- Modern cult-exit moved decisively from forced deprogramming to voluntary
  exit counseling post-1980s based on outcome data

### Refinement R-3: Screening requirement is empirically vindicated

- Pfeifer 1994: 100% of n=343 met DSM criteria
- Gallagher: ≥90% of referrals psychiatric
- Van Duijl Uganda: majority of "possessed" met DID/PTSD criteria
- Delmonte 2016: case converted from DID-positive to DID-negative through
  religious-ritual community structure — same phenomenology, different framing

### Refinement R-4: Communal coherence field is empirically grounded

- Konvalinka et al. 2011 PNAS: autonomic synchrony in collective ritual
- EEG/fNIRS hyperscanning: alpha-band inter-brain coherence in cooperative
  action
- Aronoff Lynn Malinoski 2000: social-support quality predicts recovery
- DID treatment outcome literature: therapeutic-alliance quality predicts
  outcome

### Refinement R-5: REBUS / entropic-brain as the neural mapping target

Carhart-Harris's REBUS model is the closest pre-existing neuroscience
framework to BPR's "re-coherencing" of identity winding:

- Ego ≈ DMN-organized entropy minimization
- Ritual ≈ transient entropy elevation, prior relaxation
- Recovery ≈ DMN re-canalization with revised, less rigid priors
- BPR identity winding W[φ_mind] ↔ DMN attractor structure
- BPR permission-field re-gating ↔ revised priors after re-canalization

This gives the framework five measurable observables for identity-winding
re-coherence:

1. **DMN functional connectivity shifts** pre/post intervention (mPFC–PCC
   coupling) — fMRI
2. **Frontal theta phase consistency** — EEG (Lutz & Davidson 2009)
3. **EEG Lempel-Ziv / entropy** — pre vs post intervention (Carhart-Harris)
4. **Alpha-band inter-brain coherence** during collective intervention —
   hyperscanning EEG/fNIRS
5. **Superior parietal deactivation** during ritual state — SPECT/fMRI
   (Newberg)

**None have been measured in a structured Christian deliverance protocol.**
This is the framework's most concrete research target.

## 4. What the calibration does NOT establish

1. **Does not validate any specific exorcism case** — controlled outcome
   trials for ritual deliverance do not exist
2. **Does not prove demons exist** — possession-form phenomenology has the
   same neural signature as dissociation; the framework cannot mechanically
   distinguish them
3. **Does not endorse one tradition over another** — the structural
   convergence is across traditions, not specific to Catholic Christianity
4. **Does not equate cooperative misalignment with evil** — same caveat as
   the parent `MORAL_PHENOMENOLOGY_PRIVATION.md`: cult exit, gender
   identity development, religious deconversion, abuse-survivor exit all
   look like "winding misalignment" with a prior network but are not
   identity hijacking
5. **Does not displace medical or psychiatric care** — the screening
   requirement is mechanically necessary, empirically vindicated, and the
   framework requires it
