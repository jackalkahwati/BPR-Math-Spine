# Coercive Audio — Empirical Calibration

> **Purpose:** Anchor the framework's "engineered decoherence via music"
> hypothesis to the peer-reviewed empirical record on coercive auditory
> environments, acoustic weapons, music-induced trance, engineered
> commercial audio, and the actual evidence for subliminal/specific-frequency
> claims.
> **Method:** Five-angle literature survey (May 2026).
> **Parent docs:** [`MORAL_PHENOMENOLOGY_PRIVATION.md`](../MORAL_PHENOMENOLOGY_PRIVATION.md),
> [`EXORCISM_BPR_MAPPING.md`](../EXORCISM_BPR_MAPPING.md),
> [`MORAL_PHENOMENOLOGY_EMPIRICAL_CALIBRATION.md`](MORAL_PHENOMENOLOGY_EMPIRICAL_CALIBRATION.md)

## 1. Headline finding

**Engineered acoustic decoherence is real, but the mechanism in the
documented record is gross, not subtle.** The framework's prediction
that music can be designed to drive identity-state instability is
empirically vindicated for a specific class of cases — most cleanly
casino soundscapes and post-9/11 detention "torture playlists" — but
the actual mechanism in the peer-reviewed record is **loudness + duration
+ repetition + cultural targeting + combination with other stressors**,
not mathematically-tuned waveform optimization, subliminal messaging,
or precision frequency targeting.

The popular intuition that decoherence is hidden in subtle acoustic
engineering (specific frequencies, subliminal content, hypersonic
components, infrasonic weapons) is largely **pseudoscientific or
unreplicated** in the rigorous peer-reviewed record. The decoherence
that does happen is happening through obvious mechanical means in
contexts where the listener cannot exit.

This substantially refines the detection device design: the load-bearing
detector is **deployment context + listener physiology**, not acoustic
analysis alone.

### C. Music-induced trance and dissociation neuroscience

| Finding | Source | Significance |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Becker *Deep Listeners* (2004) and Rouget *Music and Trance* (1985): trance is a learned, culturally scaffolded behavior — no acoustic feature universally produces trance; meaning is supplied by cultural framing | Becker (Indiana UP); Rouget (Chicago) | The same acoustics produce different states across cultures and consent contexts |
| Neher 1962 auditory-driving hypothesis: replications show suggestion/expectation, NOT the acoustic signal alone, predict trance | Neher; subsequent EEG replications | Acoustic-deterministic models of trance are refuted |

| Wilkins 2014 (*Sci Rep* 4:6130): DMN connectivity is **highest** during preferred music; precuneus reintegrates — DMN engaged, not silenced. | Taruffi 2017 (*Sci Rep*): DMN modulation is bidirectional and emotion-dependent; adaptive aesthetic experience | [PMC5663956](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC5663956/) | Effects are integrative, not pathological |
| Aparicio-Terrés et al. 2025 ANYAS: rhythmic acoustic ASC overlaps neurobiologically with psychedelic states (DMN modulation, altered self-referential) — characterized as **absorption/relaxation, not pathology** | [PMC12412719](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12412719/) | The pathological-DMN-suppression case is NOT standard music listening |
| Binaural beats: meta-analyses (Garcia-Argibay 2019; Ingendoh 2023 PLOS ONE) show small, inconsistent effects; brain-entrainment hypothesis not robustly supported | [PMID 30073406](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/30073406/); [PMC10198548](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10198548/) | The "binaural beats reprogram brain" claim is overstated |
| **No peer-reviewed neuroimaging documents sustained DMN suppression from non-consensual auditory exposure with iatrogenic effects** | Cross-cutting | The direct biomarker for the framework's decoherence prediction does not exist |

### E. Music in cults + subliminal research

| Finding | Source | Significance |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Music in cult contexts is always **adjunct** to isolation, sleep deprivation, in-group/out-group framing, authority — **no peer-reviewed study isolates music as an independent coercive mechanism** | Singer & Lalich 1995; Lifton 1981; ICSA literature | Music alone is not sufficient; coercion is contextual |
| Gao et al. 2017, Perry et al. 2025 *WIREs Cog Sci*: chanting effects depend on **volition, expectancy, and meaning** — the same acoustic stimulus in a coerced context is not equivalent | [PMC5223166](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC5223166/) | **The acoustic structure does not distinguish contemplative from coercive use** |

| Greenwald et al. 1991 double-blind subliminal self-help tapes (mislabeled vs correctly labeled): **only placebo/expectancy effects** | [PDF](https://faculty.washington.edu/agg/pdf/Gwald_Spang_Pratk_Esk_PsychSci_1991.OCR.pdf) | Subliminal audio: placebo, not signal |
| Shanks et al. 2020 preregistered subliminal-anchoring replications **failed** | [PMC7567524](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC7567524/) | Modern preregistered work confirms the null |
| Reverse speech (Oates): pseudoscience; no linguistic basis, demand characteristics, failed empirical tests | Newbrook & Curtain 1998 | Reverse-speech claims have no scientific basis |
| Audio steganography: **technically real for machine extraction; no peer-reviewed evidence the human brain decodes embedded content into behavior** | MDPI 2025 review | Steganography ≠ neural-level reception |
| Solfeggio frequencies (396 / 528 Hz etc.) / 432 Hz claims: **specific-frequency claims have zero empirical basis**. Any pleasant slow music produces similar effects | Calamassi & Pomponi 2019; SMU 2023 review | Numerological frequency claims are pseudoscientific |
| Hypersonic Effect (Oohashi 2000, 2006): Meyer & Moran 2007 ABX trials (n=554) found no perceptual discrimination; subjective-preference claim failed; EEG effects contested | Wikipedia summary; Fukushima/Kuribayashi 2017 | The most-cited "hidden audio effect on brain" claim has poor replication |

## 3. The headline structural finding

**Music can be engineered for decoherence — and the engineering is not subtle.**

Across all five literatures, the documented mechanisms are:

1. **Loudness above some threshold** (~85 dB sustained, much higher in detention contexts)
2. **Duration of exposure** (hours to weeks in coercive contexts)
3. **Repetition** (Barney theme on loop is the canonical case)
4. **Cultural targeting** (heavy metal/rap to violate religious/cultural sensibilities)
5. **Combination with other stressors** (sleep deprivation, isolation, sensory attack)
6. **Forced exposure / inability to exit**
7. **Acoustic deception** (LDW celebratory audio in casinos)

What is **not** documented in the rigorous record:

1. Precision waveform optimization for decoherence
2. Specific frequencies (Solfeggio, 432 Hz, etc.) with specific effects
3. Subliminal messaging affecting behavior
4. Hypersonic / ultrasonic hidden content affecting brain state at meaningful magnitude
5. Acoustic-weapon-class frequencies selectively disrupting DMN/executive networks below pain/damage thresholds
6. Backward masking / reverse-speech content embedding

The popular "subtle decoherence engineering" hypothesis is largely
unsupported. The decoherence that does happen is happening through
mechanical means in contexts where the listener cannot exit.

## 4. The DMN-engagement finding — important refinement to the framework

The original framework drafted "DMN suppression" as a marker of identity-
winding instability under coercive music. The neuroscience literature
substantially refines this:

- **Wilkins 2014:** DMN connectivity is *highest* during preferred music
- **Janata 2009:** familiar autobiographical music recruits DMN hub
- **Taruffi 2017:** DMN modulation by music is bidirectional and
  emotion-dependent — adaptive aesthetic experience
- **Aparicio-Terrés 2025:** rhythmic acoustic ASC characterized as
  **absorption/relaxation, not pathology**

**Music typically engages DMN in integrative ways (flow, absorption,
self-referential coherence), not destabilizes it.** Pathological DMN
disruption appears in disorders of consciousness, severe psychosis,
high-dose psychedelics — not in standard music listening.

This means the framework's detection device should NOT treat DMN
deactivation as a decoherence marker. It should look for the *opposite*:
**DMN dis-integration in a context where the listener does not want
to be in that state**, plus autonomic activation (cortisol, HRV collapse)
that diverges from the listener's self-reported experience (per Wagner
2024).

The discriminator is **consent + listener-reported state vs measured
physiological state**, not the acoustic profile alone.

## 5. Refinements to the detection device design

### R-1: Layer A (acoustic analysis) is necessary but insufficient

Acoustic features (loudness, dynamic range, repetition, theta-band rhythm,
dissonance, surprise events) are valid signals but **cannot distinguish
coercive from contemplative or absorptive use**. Industrial music,
shamanic drumming, harsh noise, free jazz, Sufi qawwali, Hesychast prayer,
and Tibetan Buddhist chant all score high on most "decoherence" acoustic
markers. They are not coercion.

Layer A should be used to flag concerning *profiles* for further
investigation, not to classify audio as coercive.

### R-2: Layer C (deployment context) is empirically the load-bearing detector

The convergent finding across torture, cult, and trance literatures is
that the same acoustic stimulus is decohering in coerced context and
benign/integrative in voluntary context. Layer C variables matter most:

- Is exposure forced / extended / inescapable?
- Is exposure combined with other stressors (sleep deprivation, isolation,
  sensory attack, captivity)?
- Is the listener able to exit at will?
- Is the content targeted at the listener's cultural/personal
  vulnerabilities?
- Is the exposure paired with information control or identity attacks?

### R-3: Layer B (listener physiology) is the only direct decoherence signal

The Wagner 2024 cortisol-without-conscious-stress finding is the key
diagnostic insight: the listener's conscious experience can diverge from
their physiological state. The detection device's most valuable
measurement is the **gap between self-report and biomarker**:

- Cortisol elevation during music the listener says they enjoy
- HRV collapse during music the listener says is calming
- Frontal theta/alpha disorganization the listener does not subjectively
  register
- DMN dis-integration in a context where the listener has not consented
  to ego-dissolution

This gap is what an honest detection device should surface, and it is
listener-specific.

### R-4: The framework's "engineered for decoherence" detector should NOT include subliminal/precision-frequency content checks

Adding detectors for backward masking, Solfeggio frequencies, hypersonic
components, or subliminal embedding would lend scientific credibility to
claims the rigorous literature has refuted or failed to replicate. Such
detectors would produce false positives on art and false legitimacy for
pseudoscientific claims.

### R-5: Casino-soundscape detection is the most empirically grounded use case

The Dixon LDW research and Schüll's machine-zone work give the cleanest
evidence base for an engineered acoustic environment producing measurable
dissociation and autonomic deception. A detection device validated on
casino-style soundscapes (LDW celebratory audio + sustained tempo +
sound-on-all-outcomes) would be operating on well-documented mechanism
and replicated effect sizes. Extension to less-evidenced cases should
be conservative.

## 6. What this calibration does NOT establish

1. **Does not validate claims of subliminal manipulation in commercial
   music.** Most such claims (specific frequencies, hidden messages,
   "frequency healing/harming") are pseudoscientific or unreplicated.
2. **Does not endorse music censorship.** Industrial, experimental, harsh
   noise, free jazz, and ritual musics all score high on Layer A markers
   without being coercion. A device that flags these is a censorship
   apparatus, not a protection tool.
3. **Does not establish a precision decoherence weapon exists.** The
   acoustic-weapons literature does not document one; LRAD is a pain
   device, MEDUSA was never deployed, infrasound weapons are physically
   constrained.
4. **Does not adjudicate any specific deployment** as coercive or benign.
   That requires combined Layer A + B + C assessment of the specific
   listener-audio-context triple.

## 7. Concrete research targets

| Test | What it would measure | Status |
|------|----------------------|--------|
| Replication of Wagner 2024 cortisol/conscious-stress gap across genres | Listener-specific physiological/subjective divergence under different music | Single study; not replicated |
| EEG/fMRI of survivors of documented torture playlists during exposure to the same playlists | Sustained DMN dis-integration vs control music | Not done; ethics complex; would need volunteer survivors |
| Preregistered casino-soundscape Layer B physiological measurement at scale | Whether the Dixon LDW autonomic deception generalizes across listeners | Single-lab work; field-scale replication needed |
| Hyperscanning EEG in cult-recovery group contexts vs charismatic religious contexts | Whether decoherence-promoting vs coherence-promoting communal music produces different inter-brain coherence | Not done |
| Longitudinal study of audio environment (workplace, retail, commute) on personality stability measures | Whether chronic exposure to high-Layer-A audio environments shifts the person from baseline | Not done; high research value |
