## Physical Interpretation of Key Formulas

### m_t = v_EW/√2
The top quark couples to the Higgs with maximal overlap (highest l-mode). In the boundary-to-bulk map, y_t = 1/√2.

### m_b = m_t × (E_b/c_t) × (2 + 1/(3 ln p))
- **E_b/c_t**: Down-type eigenvalue ratio (winding-shifted l(l+W_c) vs l²).
- **Factor 2**: Higgs doublet isospin structure (down-type couples through conjugate component).
- **1/(3 ln p)**: Boundary phase-space correction.

### |V_cb| = √(m_s/m_b) / √(ln(p) + z/3)
Fritzsch texture √(m_s/m_b) ≈ 0.15 is 3.7× too large. Boundary correlation length √(ln(p) + z/3) suppresses the 2–3 generation overlap.

### |V_ub| = √(m_u/m_t)
Mass-hierarchy scaling from boundary overlap.

### m_τ = v_EW × α
The tau mass is set by the electroweak scale times the fine structure constant. The lepton sector couples through both the Higgs VEV and the EM boundary.

### δ_CP = π/2 − 1/√(z+1)
CP phase from boundary coordination: the 1/√(z+1) term encodes the overlap phase between boundary modes.
