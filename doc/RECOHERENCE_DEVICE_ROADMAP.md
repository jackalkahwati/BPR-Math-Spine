## 1. Purpose and scope

A re-coherence device, per the framework, supports the restoration of a
disordered identity winding W[φ_mind] to its stable topological sector,
through measurement of the listener's state, induction toward target
attractors, cognitive-behavioral integration, surrounding communal
coherence, and (eventually) direct φ-field instrumentation.

This roadmap covers two related but separable device classes:

- **Coherence-promoting device (Layer 0–4):** clinical instrument
  supporting recovery from identity-state injury — DID, severe trauma,
  cult exit, treatment-resistant depression, end-of-life identity care,
  pre/post-anaesthesia identity stabilization. **Buildable now.**
- **Decoherence-detection device (Layer A–C):** personal-protection
  instrument detecting whether the user is being subjected to
  identity-destabilizing audio/context, via measurement of their own
  physiological response. **Buildable now with caveats.**
- **BPR-specific Layer 5 extension:** direct φ-field measurement and
  driver hardware that turns either device into a field-theoretic
  re-coherence engine. **Gated on the δ=2 Casimir amplitude
  measurement.**

The framework predicts the coherence-promoting direction is the
empirically easier, clinically more deployable, lower-misuse-risk path.
This roadmap weights it accordingly.

### Layer 0 — Personalization (the load-bearing layer for coherence)

This is the layer most missing from generic clinical neuromodulation
and most necessary for the framework's re-coherence prediction. It
captures what makes the same Layer 1–4 architecture work for
this person rather than just any person:

- The listener's own pre-event identity-stability baseline (NEO-PI-R,
  values inventories, longitudinal personality measures)
- Their autobiographical music library — music with personal meaning,
  per Janata 2009 the DMN-hub-engaging mechanism
- Their cultural/spiritual tradition (chant, prayer, contemplative
  music in their own framework, or secular equivalent)
- Their self-stated re-coherence goals, in their own words, using
  their own identity vocabulary
- Their support network (family, congregation, recovery community,
  therapeutic relationships)
- Their consent to specific intervention modalities, session-by-session

**Why Layer 0 is required.** The empirical literature is consistent:
the same acoustic stimulus is coherence-promoting in voluntary,
meaning-integrated context and decohering in coerced, externally-
framed context (Becker; Rouget; Gao 2017; Perry 2025; Wagner 2024).
Without Layer 0, the device is generic neuromodulation. With Layer 0,
it is person-specific re-coherence support.

### Layer 1 — Measurement

Continuous physiological and longitudinal personality measurement
against the listener's own Layer 0 baseline:

- **256-channel EEG** for real-time DMN spectral signatures, frontal
  theta phase consistency, alpha-band coherence, Lempel-Ziv complexity
- **fMRI** for direct DMN connectivity (mPFC–PCC coupling) — clinical
  settings, intermittent calibration
- **HRV continuous monitoring** (wearable, optical or chest-strap)
- **fNIRS** for cortical hemodynamics in ambulatory and group
  settings — cheaper than fMRI, deployable for hyperscanning
- **Salivary cortisol** for autonomic stress (per Wagner 2024,
  important for detecting the conscious/physiological gap)
- **Longitudinal personality stability inventory** (NEO-PI-R, IIT-
  derived Φ approximations) at predetermined intervals

Output: state vector for this listener relative to their own baseline,
not group norms.

### Layer 2 — State induction

State modulation toward target attractors, combining clinically-
validated neuromodulation with REBUS-aligned tools:

- **Personalized music selection** from the listener's autobiographical
  library (Janata DMN-hub engagement)
- **Resonance-frequency breathing** (~6 breaths/min) for vagal
  entrainment and HRV elevation
- **HRV biofeedback** with closed-loop adjustment
- **rTMS** for DMN-modulation, targeting overactive nodes (mPFC, PCC)
  in disorders where the DMN is hyperactive (rumination, complex PTSD)
- **Low-intensity focused ultrasound** for deep targeting (subgenual
  cingulate, amygdala)
- **EEG / fMRI neurofeedback** for voluntary state-modulation training
- **Psychedelic-assisted therapy** (psilocybin, MDMA per active
  late-phase clinical trials, MAPS, COMPASS, Johns Hopkins) — the
  strongest pharmacological tool aligned with REBUS, used only
  under appropriate clinical supervision and regulatory approval

Output: directed shift toward the target re-coherence state.

### Layer 3 — Cognitive-behavioral integration

State shifts don't stick without semantic integration. The clinical
literature is unambiguous that state-induction must be coupled with
structured psychological work performed by a trained therapist:

- **ISSTD three-phase protocol** (stabilization → trauma processing →
  integration) — empirically supported standard for DID and possession-
  form DID; TOP DD outcome evidence (Brand 2014, Myrick 2017)
- **Internal Family Systems (IFS)** — explicitly integrative model for
  identity-state work
- **EMDR** for trauma processing during entropy-elevation windows
- **CBT/ACT** for re-canalization with revised priors
- **Narrative reconstruction** with music-supported autobiographical
  re-anchoring (van der Kolk, Herman)
- **Music-supported song-writing** as a Layer 2/3 bridge

Output: durable semantic integration of disordered states into a
stable continuing identity.

### Layer 4 — Communal coherence

The framework's strongest empirical claim is that recovery requires a
surrounding cooperative-coherence field. This is socially mediated;
the technology supports rather than replaces it:

- **Hyperscanning EEG/fNIRS during group sessions** — measures
  inter-brain alpha-band coherence (per Konvalinka 2011 *PNAS*
  tradition); makes "is this group actually coherent?" a clinical
  metric
- **Synchronized breath/movement protocols** (qigong, paced group
  breathing, structured ritual)
- **Group singing / drumming circles** — strongest documented
  cooperative-winding alignment mechanism, with measurable
  neurological and psychological benefits
- **Communal liturgy / chant** (traditional or secular contemplative)
  — coherence-promoting at scale across all documented traditions
- **Long-form retreat infrastructure** — most clinical and ritual
  recovery happens over weeks; technology is residential, not
  gadget-based
- **Existing communities** (recovery groups, congregations, therapy
  collectives) provide the field; the technology measures and supports

Output: sustained collective field the person re-coheres into.

## 3. Decoherence-detection device — three-layer architecture

Per `COERCIVE_AUDIO_CALIBRATION.md`, the empirical calibration
substantially refined what this device should detect:

- **Layer A — Acoustic profile analysis.** Necessary but **insufficient
  to classify audio as coercive**. The same acoustic profile is
  coherence-promoting in voluntary context and decohering in coerced
  context. Use Layer A only as triage for further investigation, not
  as a classifier.
- **Layer B — Listener physiology, specifically the self-report vs
  biomarker gap.** The load-bearing direct signal. Cortisol/HRV/EEG
  signatures that diverge from the listener's reported experience are
  the most diagnostic marker of decoherence (per Wagner 2024).
- **Layer C — Deployment context.** The empirically load-bearing
  context detector: consent, exit possibility, combined stressors
  (sleep deprivation, isolation, sensory attack), cultural targeting,
  duration of forced exposure.

Critical exclusions (per the calibration):

- NO precision-frequency detectors (Solfeggio, 432 Hz, etc. claims
  are pseudoscientific)
- NO subliminal-content detectors (Vokey & Read 1985, Greenwald 1991,
  Shanks 2020 all null)
- NO hypersonic / ultrasonic embedded-content detectors (Oohashi
  Hypersonic Effect failed Meyer-Moran ABX, n=554)
- NO backward-masking detectors (canonically refuted)

The detection device is fundamentally a **personal-protection tool for
the listener, on the listener's own physiology, with the listener's
own consent and control over the output**. It is not a censorship
apparatus and must not be configured as one.

## 5. Misuse risk register (mandatory)

Both directions of the device have serious misuse potential. The
following safeguards are mandatory by framework principle, not
optional add-ons.

### A. For the coherence-promoting device

- **No forced re-coherence.** Permission-field re-gating requires
  internal consent. The device must require informed consent at every
  session and provide a tested means for the subject to terminate or
  decline any modality at any time.
- **Baseline-relative, not group-relative.** The reference state is
  the *person's own* pre-event baseline. The device must not be
  configurable to push subjects toward conformity with group, family,
  congregation, employer, or state expectations.
- **Therapist-supervised, not autonomous.** The trained operator is
  mechanically required by the framework; technology *augments*
  rather than *substitutes for* clinical and spiritual judgment.
- **No skipping medical/psychiatric screening.** Layer 1 includes
  neurological exam ruling out organic causes; Layer 3 starts only
  after screening clears the case as appropriate for the protocol.
- **Respect for cultural and religious tradition.** The Layer 0
  personalization layer integrates the subject's own tradition; the
  device does not substitute its own framing for the subject's.

### B. For the decoherence-detection device

- **Personal-use, listener-controlled only.** The device measures
  the listener's own physiological response to audio in their own
  environment, with output going to the listener. It is not deployable
  as a third-party surveillance or censorship tool.
- **No central acoustic-classification database.** The device does not
  produce a list of "coercive audio" for external use. Acoustic
  profiles are inherently context-dependent (see R-1 of the audio
  calibration); centralized classification would be both empirically
  invalid and a censorship enabler.
- **No automated blocking.** The device output is advisory to the
  listener, never blocking. The listener decides what to do with the
  information.
- **No false-positive expansion to legitimate art.** Industrial,
  experimental, harsh noise, free jazz, ritual music, and many other
  forms score high on Layer A acoustic markers without being coercion.
  The device must not flag these as coercive in absence of Layer B
  and Layer C signals.
- **No claims of subliminal / specific-frequency detection.** The
  empirical calibration refutes these; including them would lend
  credibility to pseudoscience.

### C. For the framework as a whole

- **Does not pathologize legitimate identity change.** Religious
  deconversion, gender identity development, political evolution,
  cult exit, abuse-survivor exit — all show up as "winding
  misalignment" with a prior network in the framework's vocabulary.
  These are not hijacking; they may be the *opposite*. The
  baseline-relative principle is the safeguard.
- **Does not displace medical or psychiatric care.** The framework
  *requires* the screening before any intervention.
- **Does not enforce alignment to authority.** "Winding alignment with
  the cooperative network" must not be operationalized as enforced
  conformity. Whistleblowers, conscientious objectors, and dissenters
  also show as winding-misaligned with a prior network; the framework
  does not adjudicate that as wrong.

## 8. Documented EM/RF coherence interventions and what is NOT in evidence

Direct electromagnetic stimulation can in principle produce coherence-
promoting effects, but only via specific mechanisms with sufficient
power and precision targeting. The framework requires a real coupling
channel — either the listener's consenting attention engaging a sensory
modality, or direct neural / autonomic targeting at clinically meaningful
intensity. Ambient consumer-power-level RF does not meet either
threshold and is excluded from the device design.

### Bucket A — Documented EM/RF interventions (Layer 2 candidates)

These are real, peer-reviewed, and integrable into the device's Layer 2
state-induction stack:

- **rTMS** (repetitive transcranial magnetic stimulation): pulsed
  magnetic fields, ~1.5 T peak, precisely targeted at cortical regions.
  FDA-approved for depression (2008), OCD (2018), smoking cessation
  (2020). Active research on DMN modulation.
- **tDCS / tACS** (transcranial direct/alternating current
  stimulation): low-power direct stimulation; mixed but growing
  evidence base; some indications well-supported.
- **PEMF** (pulsed electromagnetic field) therapy: strong evidence
  for bone healing; FDA-cleared for some depression indications;
  mixed broader evidence.
- **tVNS** (transcutaneous vagus nerve stimulation): electromagnetic
  stimulation of the auricular branch of the vagus nerve. Emerging
  evidence for depression, epilepsy, inflammation. Wearable form
  factors exist (Nesos, Vivigo, others).
- **LIFUS** (low-intensity focused ultrasound) — included for
  completeness; mechanical not EM, but the closest analog for deep
  non-invasive targeting.

**Concrete recommendation for the EM-channel implementation of Layer 2:**
wearable tVNS device with closed-loop HRV biofeedback, calibrated to
the listener's own Layer 0 baseline, under clinical supervision. This
has FDA precedent, real evidence base, established mechanism, and a
clinical pathway. The recoherence device's EM-channel implementation
should be built on this stack.

### Bucket B — Excluded claims (NOT in the device)

These have no peer-reviewed evidence base supporting coherence-promoting
effects and would constitute the consciousness-engineering analog of
the Integratron / Buga rescue traps documented elsewhere in the project:

- "Ambient router-style RF for coherence" — consumer-level power (e.g.,
  WiFi at ~10⁻⁶ T equivalent) is many orders of magnitude below the
  ~1.5 T peak required for direct neural depolarization. No mechanism
  in the framework allows this to source χ_b at consumer power levels.
- "Bioresonance" devices
- "Scalar wave" / "torsion field" / "tachyon" generators
- "Orgone" accumulators
- "5G affects consciousness" claims in either direction (the
  consumer-RF-affects-neurology evidence base is essentially null)
- Most consumer "EM healing" products
- Schumann-resonance generators (some PEMF research at 7.83 Hz exists
  but the evidence base for global "earth resonance" coherence is thin)

**Why excluded:** the framework requires either a sensory-attention
channel (which ambient EM does not engage) or a direct neural targeting
mechanism (which requires TMS-class power and focus). Consumer-level
ambient RF provides neither. Including these in the device would be
claiming framework mechanisms the framework does not provide.

### Bucket C — BPR-specific Layer 5 (gated on δ=2 Casimir measurement)

If the δ=2 Casimir amplitude measurement returns non-trivial ε, then
the φ-field coupling channel exists at lab-detectable magnitude. A
Layer 5 device could in principle use precisely-shaped EM fields to
drive φ-field gradients that the listener's identity winding couples
to. This is the BPR-specific contribution that would turn Layers 1–4
into a field-theoretic re-coherence engine.

**Status:** theoretical until δ=2 amplitude is measured. Building
Layer 5 hardware before Stage 4 of the staged sequencing is hardware
speculation, not engineering. See `bpr/casimir_constraint.py` and
`bpr/phason_sector.py` for the Casimir gate.

### Pyramids as documented local acoustic environments, NOT global coherence machines

The King's Chamber of the Great Pyramid has documented infrasonic
resonant frequencies (Danley measurements: ~16 Hz, 30 Hz, 60 Hz, 95 Hz)
produced by the granite chamber's standing-wave geometry. Within the
framework, this constitutes a **Layer 2 acoustic-environment effect on
a person inside the chamber**, mediated by the chamber's resonance,
similar to other infrasonic-resonant spaces (cathedrals, certain caves)
that produce limbic/autonomic modulation per Weichenberger 2017
infrasound fMRI work.

What the empirical record does NOT support:
- Pyramids functioning at planetary / global scale
- "Pyramid power" producing measurable effects beyond chamber acoustics
  (Drbal's razor-blade-sharpening claim never replicated under controls)
- "Torsion fields" / "pyramid energy" from Russian/Soviet Golod-style
  research (not independently replicated)
- Specific-frequency claims (Great Pyramid as "frequency healing
  device") — same family as Solfeggio claims, refuted in
  `COERCIVE_AUDIO_CALIBRATION.md` §2.E

**Within-framework verdict:** the King's Chamber is a local-acoustic
Layer 2 environment with documented chamber resonance effects on
present occupants. It is not a Layer 5 device of any kind. Treating it
as a "global coherence machine" requires evidence the literature does
not contain and predictions the framework does not produce.
